Project

Air Quality Index Prediction:

Air quality index (AQI) is a quantitative tool through which air pollution is measured,
providing information on how clean or polluted the air is. Several countries employ
some type of air quality index to communicate the quality of their air. Some of these
systems rely on measured (monitored) or predicted concentrations of air pollutants
(results of modeling systems). In this project, a methodology for developing an air
quality index (AQI). This index is a national air quality rating system based on the
national ambient air quality standards.
